**Suresh I. S. Rattan**  
**Abstract** Various dietary components present in spices, herbs, and numerous medicinal plants, and some vitamins, trace elements, minerals and others are potential hormetins which can have health beneficial, anti-ageing and longevity promoting effects. These nutritional hormetins have almost no nutritional value in terms of providing proteins, carbohydrates and fats, but have pleiotropic biological effects in maintaining, recovering, and enhancing health and longevity.
